Week1 Lab

This week's lab is quite simple though it is important that you get the components installed correctly. We will be installing R and the RStudio IDE

To install R for MS-Windows:

  • Proceed to HERE and click the "Download R 3.1.2 for Windows" link
  • Once the download has been completed double-click the installer and follow the directions

To install R for Apple OSX:

  • Proceed to HERE and click the "R-3.1.2.pkg (latest version)" link
  • Once the download has been completed double-click the installer and follow the directions

To install RStudio:

  • Proceed to HERE and click the "Download RStudio Desktop" icon
  • You will then be presented with a page that points you to the relevant link for your OS.
  • Once the download has been completed double-click the installer and follow the directions
